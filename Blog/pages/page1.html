THE ART ADVISOR

Encouraging The Art of Viewing Art



Mariana Del Nogal is a rising junior at Parsons The New School for Design who is pursuing a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in Art History. Despite her focus towards design, the student has developed a keen interest for fine arts, art advising and the art market, and how such fields subsequently engage with social media outlets.

The Art Advisor started as a blog dedicated to showcase exclusive works in Art, Architecture, and Fashion of emerging artists and designers within the Parsons community. This project encouraged students to suggest possible content for the account by using the hashtag #ParsonsPride on Instagram.

Short after this initiative, The Art Advisor project extended into an online platform that uniquely features Fine Art expositions displayed throughout the city of New York, hence providing the latest insights pertaining to the art world.

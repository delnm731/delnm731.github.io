ABOUT


The Art Advisor started as a blog dedicated to showcase exclusive works in Art, Architecture, and Fashion of emerging artists and designers within the Parsons community. Students could also suggest possible content for the site by commenting #ParsonsPride on Instagram.

Short after this initiative, The Art Advisor project extended into an online magazine that uniquely features Fine Art expositions displayed throughout New York City, hence providing the latest insights pertaining to the art world.
